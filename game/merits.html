Merits & Flaws

The merit and flaw system is a way for you to customize your character in a way that is unique and unlike others. Merits are ways to receive bonuses for different rolls and checks based on a certain situation. For each merit you select you will need to select a flaw to match. Merits and flaws are broken down into three categories. If you choose a category 3 merit you must choose a category 3 flaw. In addition, some merits cost build points in order to take. You can earn build points by simply playing the game.

Each merit from category 3 costs 500bp to choose. This will effectively limit you to making 2 choices from category 3.

Other Merits

Each of the following merits can be chosen. They will each cost some amount of build points and require you to pick 1 category 1 flaw.

Shield Wall - You know how to work with others to form a shield wall, giving a bonus to shields if someone else in your party also has shield wall. Cost: 100bp
Favoured Enemy - Available enemies include wolves, wargs, bears, boars, warthogs, uruks, trolls, and undead. You will receive a +5 to any hostile actions toward those enemies. You may only have 1 favoured enemy. Cost: 50bp
Combat Specialist - This merit will give you a +5 to one combat skill of your choice. This makes you a specialist in your chosen combat skill. You may choose: Daggers, Light-Blade, Long-Blade, Heavy-Blade, Light-Bludgeon, Heavy-Bludgeon, Bludgeon, Light-Axe, Axe, Heavy-Axe, Short-Spear, Long-Spear, Throwing, Shortbow, Longbow, Parry, Dodge, Shields. Cost: 150bp
