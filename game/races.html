Playable Races

Men and women of Gondor lived in the lands of Gondor before the realm of Gondor was founded. Middle Men sore allegience to the Dúnedain of Gondor upon the founding of the kingdom, placing them under the rule of the king of Gondor. The humans who once roamed Middle-Earth would intermarry and eventually become one with the Dúnedain of Gondor. The men and women who are of true gondorian descent are typically tall with pale skin and dark hair.


This is the starting race for those who choose to enter Arda Marred. There are no benefits or drawbacks to choosing a Gondorian Human. The race is diverse and can fill any role. Ultimately, this race is more versatile in how their personalities are developed, what skills they choose, and how they react to different situations.

Men of the North also known as Northmen dwell east of the Greenwood and other parts of Rhovanion. These men were friendly with the Dúnedain and are only second to the Dúnedain in terms of noble characteristics. At the height of Gondor's power, the Northmen flourised under the protection of the larger kingdom. With the marriage between Valancar of Gondor and the daughter of the King of Rohan Vidumavi, the allegiance between the two kingdoms solidified for but a brief time. During this era in Middle Earth, Northmen might be refered to as the Éothéod.

Bonus Stats
Adaptibility

Northmen from Rhovannion are adaptable to their training, upon creation you can select 1 additional stat to any of the following:

  • Strength
  • Constitution
  • Agility
  • Dexterity
  • Intelligence
  • Wisdom

Characteristics
Defining Features

Northern humans appear different physically from Gondorian Humans. Some key characteristics of Northmen include:

  • Masculine Features
  • Brown or Golden Brown Hair
  • Tan Skin

Languages
Learned Languages

Northmen do not have any additional languages they start with, but these languages and scripts will be available upon chargen:

  • Westron
  • Sindarin
  • Mannish Cirth

Dunlendings by Angelo Montaini

Dunlendings are tribal Hill-men that settled the mountains between areas that are west and south of the Misty Mountains as far down as the White Mountains. Dunlendings came to fear and hate men who came out of the West. That same fear and hate drove the Dunelendings to settle the grassy plains of Dunland which was west of Moria between the Misty Mountains and Isengard. Those who did not venture north to become Men of Bree settled to become Dunlendings. Arda Marred is set just before the time when Eorl drove the Dunlendings out of thier homeland.

Bonus Stats
Adaptibility

Dunlendings are tough and hardy people, you will have 2 points to spend on boosting stats (1 per stat):

  • Constitution
  • Strength
  • Agility

Characteristics
Harsh Living

Dunlendings are used to lviing in harsh conditions which have led to the following traits:

  • Leather and Fur Clothing
  • Tough and Hardy
  • Unkempt Hair and Beards

Survivability
Professions

Due to living in a hilly and forested area, agriculture is the least useful method of food production. Instead, Dunlendings might choose:

  • Hunting
  • Foraging
  • Fishing

Dwarves were created by Aule who did not wish to wait for the coming of the children of Iluvatar. Dwarves were meant to be taught the lore and crafts of Aule but an intervention by Eru caused his adoption of the dwarves. Only one name of the original Seven Fathers of dwarves is known, Durin I or Durin the Deathless. During the era of Arda Marred, Dwarves would have fled from Khazad-dûm for safety in the Grey Mountains or Erebor. Dragons began to descend upon the Grey Mountains causing some of those who lived there to flee for the Iron Hills while the remainder fled to Erebor.
Bonus Stats
Strong and Stout

Dwarves are strong and hearty, they will receive the additional bonuses to their stats upon character creation:

  • Strength
  • Constitution

Crafting
Stone and Metal

Dwarves are some of the greatest miners that ever existed. To accompany that, some of their favored crafts are:

  • Armourcraft
  • Metalcraft
  • Stonecraft
  • Weaponcraft

Languages
Khuzdûl and Angerthas

Dwarves will enter the game knowing the following languages and scripts:

  • Common Speech
  • Khuzdûl
  • Angerthas

"Elven Cloak" by Rob Alexander

Elves are the elder race in Middle Earth with many similarities to men as both were born of Eru Iluvatar. However, elves are immortal and their bodies do not visually age once they reach maturity. Elves can still die from physical injury, wasting away from malnourishment, or if they have lost the will to live. Elves are beatiful in both face and body with little difference between males and females except for means of reproduction. Elves have far better hearing than men and possess skills and abilities far beyond mankind.
